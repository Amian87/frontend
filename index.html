Background

I'm a graduate of Starter School recently completing an internship at Public Good Software in Chicago, IL. Prior to working in User Experience, I worked abroad in construction industry planning and sales. I've lived and worked in places outside of the United States and have seen the world from different perspectives. As a UX Designer, my work applies my multi-cultural outlook and entrepreneurial mindset to opportunities to create real value for users. When I craft products, I believe that they should solve problems from the perspective of the user and augment their capabilities. I'm passionate about using technology to solve problems, and to continue designing compelling products and solutions.
